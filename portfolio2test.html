XENA WONG

[Fashion Designer]

I am a fashion designer currently based in New York as I pursue my studies at Parsons School of Design in their B.F.A. Program. Since moving to the city in 2017 to attend college, I have been honing my skills as a visionary and have been working to develop my brand ever since.

My background in art and design is extensive, having been engaged in courses pertaining to pattern making and illustration at The Fashion Institute of Technology, engineering studies at Cooper Union and to even more analytical fiber science research (FSAD Summer Program) at Cornell University.
