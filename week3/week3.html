5 Ways To Improve Your Sleeping Habits

Given how much of our lives we will be spending asleep (which is approximately 33%), it is important to ensure that you master the “skill” of falling asleep and staying asleep!

Journaling

Activities such as journaling can provide an outlet to vent your frustrations and anxieties that you would normally confront later on at night before bed. Writing down your fears of the future, finances, your occupation or the current political climate can clear your head, therefore promoting better quality of rest.

Yoga

Physical activities such as exercising and yoga are great ways to release pent up anxiety or anger while also encouraging the production of endorphins. While physically combating and relieving stress at the moment, the long-term effects of endorphin production help increase mood and decrease stress that later causes sleeping issues.

Calming Music

Calming music helps lower your heart rate while also triggering the release of serotonin and melatonin—which are examples of some sleep-friendly hormones that will ensure you get the best sleep of your life. Give it a try by clicking the video below!

Calming Music for Sleep, Meditation and Relaxation

Aromatherapy

Aromatherapy helps relax the body and mind by releasing natural plant extracts into the air to promote health and well-being. It is helpful for unwinding and relieving any stress or tension before a night of rest.

Establish A Routine

Establishing a routine allows for a chance to relax/calm down before bed and immediately after waking. A consistent schedule helps you regulate your body’s clock and can help promote sleep as well as staying asleep throughout the night.
